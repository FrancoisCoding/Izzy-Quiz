Friends (The TV Show) Quiz!

Welcome to Manhattan… Home of Ross, Rachel, Phoebe, Chandler, Joey, and Monica! The Friends TV Show follows 6 best friends as they go through life together in New York City. Let’s go through each Friend and then see how much you know about this tv show!

Ross Geller is obsessed with science, a total nerd, and a very competitive person. He is known for going through many divorces and being in love with Rachel Green. He was not the coolest kid in High School but has done very well with himself in the professional world. He is a very smart guy and can sometimes be known as the know it all.

Rachel Greene is the girl that everyone dreams of being with. She is very laid back, fashionable, loyal, and not the best cooker. She is known to never truly know what she wants but still strives hard to get what she wants. She is a trendsetter and is known to have the best haircuts throughout the whole tv show.

Phoebe Buffay is a sweet-natured but odd guitar player. Her most known written song is Smelly Cat. She never had a normal or complete childhood but is still a child at heart. She believes Santa Claus still existed until Joey told her otherwise. She is a free-spirited soul who loves animals and life with all of her heart. She ends up breaking up with one of her lovers due to him shooting a pigeon that was annoying him on his roof.

Chandler Bing is the class clown of the whole group. There is never one conversation that he doesn’t let a joke or sarcastic comment slip. He is known to be Joey’s roommate and the more serious of the pair. He has a great fear of commitment and is known throughout the tv show to be always connected to his lover/ex-lover/lover again, Janice. You can always count on Chandler to laugh!

Nothing explains Joey Tribbiani like a meatball sandwich loving guy. He is the promiscuous one who is known to be dim-witted. He is loyal, caring, and protective of his friends. He is known to be a famous actor on the soap opera, Days of Our Lives but doesn’t have the best acting talent. He is a food-loving womanizer who has a big Italian family. His views are very child-like and he is loved by so many.

Monica Geller is a perfect representation of a Type A personality. A few things she loves are cleaning, cooking, and games. She is the “mother” of the group but also the level-headed practical friend. She is passionate, caring, and a little too competitive. She loves organization and is passionate about becoming a chef. She loves giving advice and being there for anyone of her friends when they need help.
